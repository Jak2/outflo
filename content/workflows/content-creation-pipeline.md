## Research & Ideation

Use this prompt to generate validated content ideas based on your niche and audience.

Prompt:
"I create content for [TARGET AUDIENCE] in the [NICHE] space. Generate 10 content ideas that:
- Address a specific pain point
- Have real search potential
- Can be repurposed across multiple platforms
- Are not already oversaturated

Format each idea as: Title | Pain Point | Target Keyword | Content Type"

## Outline & Structure

Turn your chosen idea into a detailed content outline before writing a single word.

Prompt:
"Create a detailed content outline for: [CONTENT TITLE]

Include:
1. Hook / opening angle
2. 5–7 main sections with subpoints
3. Key statistics or data points to find
4. Recommended real-world examples
5. CTA recommendation

Audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
Primary goal: [Educate / Sell / Build trust]"

## First Draft

Generate a complete first draft from your outline.

Prompt:
"Write a complete first draft based on this outline:

[PASTE YOUR OUTLINE HERE]

Style guidelines:
- Conversational and direct tone
- Short paragraphs (2–3 lines max)
- Use bullet points for lists
- Include smooth transitions between sections
- Target word count: [X words]"

## Edit & Optimise

Refine the draft for quality, clarity, and SEO performance.

Prompt:
"Review and improve this draft:

[PASTE DRAFT HERE]

Optimise for:
1. Clarity — remove jargon, simplify every sentence
2. Flow — improve transitions between sections
3. SEO — natural usage of target keyword: [TARGET KEYWORD]
4. Engagement — add power words and stronger hooks
5. CTA — rewrite the closing call-to-action to be more compelling"

## Repurpose & Distribute

Turn your finished article into content for every platform.

Prompt:
"Take this article and create all of the following:

1. Twitter/X thread (8–10 tweets, first tweet = hook)
2. LinkedIn post (200–300 words, personal tone)
3. Email newsletter intro (150 words, conversational)
4. 3 short-form video scripts (60 seconds each, different angles)

Original article:
[PASTE ARTICLE HERE]

Keep the core insights the same. Adapt tone and format for each platform."
